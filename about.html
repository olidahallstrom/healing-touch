Biography

A Lifelong Commitment to Healing

Dr. Lee Anna Edgerton is a native of Rutherford County, where Healing Touch Chiropractic Center is now located at 380 US-HWY 221 N, Rutherfordton, NC. She earned her Doctor of Chiropractic Degree from Sherman College of Straight Chiropractic in 1998 and has served her community with dedication ever since.

Prior to her chiropractic education, Dr. Edgerton graduated from Gardner-Webb University with a Master of Science Degree in Health Education, and earned her Bachelor of Science Degree in Public Health from the University of North Carolina at Greensboro. Beyond her formal education, she has pursued specialty training in Low Force Chiropractic Care.

Dr. Edgerton is committed to helping people realize their interconnectedness and participation with life through a greater expression of their self-healing capacity — and the organizing power within.

Specialty

What is Low Force Chiropractic Care?

Low Force Chiropractic Care is an innovative, gentle technique that achieves results without the high-velocity thrusts used in traditional chiropractic adjustments. This makes it safe and comfortable for patients of all ages and conditions.

The technique is particularly well-suited for:

  • Newborns and infants
  • Children and adolescents
  • Pregnant women
  • Elderly patients and those with osteoporosis
  • Patients with acute injuries or heightened sensitivity
  • Anyone who prefers a gentler approach
