Low Force Chiropractic Adjustments

Dr. Edgerton's signature technique uses gentle, precise methods to correct spinal misalignments without the cracking or thrusting of traditional adjustments. The spine is the window to the autonomic nervous system — keeping it aligned supports your organs, blood supply, and glands.

Low force adjustments allow the nervous system to function at its highest potential, supporting the body's natural healing process in a way that feels comfortable and reassuring.

  • Safe for newborns, infants, and children
  • Ideal for elderly patients and those with osteoporosis
  • Effective for acute and chronic conditions
  • No high-velocity thrusts or audible “cracking”
🧖

Professional Massage Therapy

Our professional massage therapy services work hand-in-hand with chiropractic care to reduce muscle tension, improve circulation, and accelerate healing. Massage therapy can significantly enhance the effectiveness of your chiropractic adjustments.

  • Relaxation and deep tissue massage
  • Trigger point and myofascial release
  • Pre- and post-chiropractic treatment massage
  • Stress relief and total body relaxation

Exercise Therapy

Therapeutic exercise is a critical component of comprehensive care. Dr. Edgerton incorporates guided exercise programs to strengthen supporting muscles, improve posture, and help patients maintain the benefits of their chiropractic care between visits.

  • Customized to your individual condition and goals
  • Core strengthening and spinal stabilization
  • Flexibility and range of motion improvement
  • Postural correction exercises
