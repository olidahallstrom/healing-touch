Business: Healing Touch Chiropractic Center
Tagline: A healthy spine is a healthy body
Address: 380 US-HWY 221 N, Rutherfordton, NC 28139
Doctor: Dr. Lee Anna Edgerton, DC

Goal:
Create a fully responsive and user-friendly website for Healing Touch Chiropractic Center. The website will include sections such as "About," "Services," "Contact," "Booking Appointments," "Testimonials," and "Blog." It will feature clean design with intuitive navigation and focus on providing a professional, trustworthy image for the chiropractor.

Features:

Home Page:

Hero section with a welcoming message, a brief overview, and call-to-action buttons for "Book Appointment" and "Learn More."

A short introduction about the chiropractor.

Featured services with icons or images for each.

Client testimonial carousel.

About Page:

Detailed bio of the chiropractor, including education, experience, and philosophy.

Optional photo gallery or video introduction.

Services Page:

A section for each chiropractic service offered, with descriptions and pricing if applicable.

A FAQ section with common patient questions.

Contact Page:

Contact form with fields for name, email, message, and phone number.

Map location of the clinic.

Phone number and email details.

Booking Page:

Online booking system integration or booking form.

Appointment scheduler that integrates with clinic’s calendar or appointment system.

Blog Section:

Simple blog with posts about chiropractic care, tips for healthy living, etc.

Design Guidelines:

Clean, modern design with a professional color scheme.

High-quality, relevant images (potentially a stock photo library for chiropractors or wellness).

Fonts that convey trust, professionalism, and accessibility.

Technology Stack:
Use HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and possibly a lightweight front-end framework like Bootstrap or Tailwind CSS. For dynamic functionality, feel free to use React.js or another modern JS framework for building interactive elements (e.g., appointment booking, dynamic FAQ, etc.).

SEO and Accessibility Considerations:

Ensure the website is SEO-friendly with meta descriptions, headings, alt tags for images, etc.

Ensure that the website is fully accessible (WCAG 2.1) with proper ARIA attributes, keyboard navigation, and contrast standards.
