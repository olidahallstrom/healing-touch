Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Lee Anna Edgerton, DC

A native of Rutherford County, Dr. Edgerton has been dedicated to her community's health since receiving her Doctor of Chiropractic Degree from Sherman College of Straight Chiropractic in 1998.

Her specialty in Low Force Chiropractic Care is an innovative, gentle technique addressing all aspects of well-being — mental, emotional, occupational, chemical, and physical.

  • Doctor of Chiropractic — Sherman College of Straight Chiropractic (1998)
  • M.S. in Health Education — Gardner-Webb University
  • B.S. in Public Health — UNC Greensboro
  • Specialty Training in Low Force Chiropractic Care
