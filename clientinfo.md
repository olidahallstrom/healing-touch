Dr. Lee Anna Edgerton, a native of Rutherford County
where her practice is now located, graduated from
Sherman College of Straight Chiropractic, receiving her
Doctor of Chiropractic Degree in 1998.  She previously
graduated from Gardner-Webb University with a Master
of Science Degree in Health Education.  She earned her
Bachelor of Science Degree in Public Health from the
University of North Carolina at Greensboro.  Beyond her
education at Sherman College she has specialty training in
Low Force Chiropractic Care.

Dr. Edgerton's specialty in low force Chiropractic Care is
an innovative, gentle technique which is effective and safe
for the care of all ages from infants to the elderly.  The
comprehensive care delivered through chiropractic
incorporates all aspects of well-being including mental,
emotional, occupational, chemical and physical.

Dr. Edgerton is committed to people realizing their
interconnectedness and participation with life through
greater expression of their self-healing capacity and
organizing the power within.  Dr. Edgerton is available to
speak to groups and organizations about Low Force
Chiropractic care and Holistic Health and healing.
